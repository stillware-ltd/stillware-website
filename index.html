Frequently Asked Questions

Is it really free forever?

Yes. If you are among the first 500 users to download Zeroed and set up your budget, your Google Drive "Universal Key" will be permanently flagged as "Founder Status." You will never be asked to pay for version 1.x features.

What is the catch? Why give it away?

We are a new app, and we need feedback more than we need money right now. In exchange for this free lifetime license, we just ask that you test our "Universal CSV Import" and "Drive Sync" and report any bugs you find.

What happens after the first 500 users?

The price will increase to $39.99 (Mobile) and $79.99 (Universal Bundle) for all new users. You will be grandfathered in and won't be affected.

How does the "Universal Cloud Key" work?

It's how we sync your license without a central server. When you install the app, we save a secure verification file to your Google Drive. Any other device (Desktop or Mobile) that connects to that Drive will see the file and instantly unlock Pro features.
