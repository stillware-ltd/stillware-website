Delete Account & Data

Zeroed is a Local-First App

Unlike traditional apps, Zeroed does not have a central account system. Your data is stored on your device, giving you complete control over your privacy and information.

How to Remove Your Data

Since Zeroed is designed with privacy in mind, removing your data is straightforward. Follow the steps below based on the features you use.

1 Local Data (On Your Device)

All your budget data, transactions, categories, and payees are stored locally on your device in an encrypted database.

To remove local data:

  • Uninstall the app – This will delete all locally stored data automatically.
  • Clear app data – Go to your device settings → Apps → Zeroed → Clear Data (or Storage) to remove all app data without uninstalling.

⚠️ Important: Once deleted, local data cannot be recovered unless you have a backup on Google Drive.

2 Google Drive Sync (If Enabled)

If you use the cross-device sync feature, an encrypted copy of your data is stored in your personal Google Drive account.

To disconnect and remove synced data:

  1. Open Zeroed and sign out from your Google account in the app settings.
  2. Visit the Google Account Permissions page to revoke Zeroed's access to your Drive.
  3. Open Google Drive and delete the ZeroedData folder to remove all synced data.

3 Purchases & Subscriptions (RevenueCat)

Your purchase history for Zeroed Pro or the Universal Bundle is managed through your App Store (iOS) or Play Store (Android) account, not directly through Zeroed.

What this means:

  • Purchase records are tied to your Apple ID or Google Play account.
  • Zeroed uses RevenueCat to verify purchases but does not store your payment information.
  • To manage or cancel subscriptions, use your device's native subscription settings.

Manage subscriptions:

  • iOS: Settings → Your Name → Subscriptions
  • Android: Google Play Store → Account → Payments & Subscriptions

4 Desktop Licenses (Lemon Squeezy)

If you purchased a desktop license, your payment was processed by Lemon Squeezy, who acts as the Merchant of Record.

To manage your desktop license:

  • Check your email for the original purchase receipt from Lemon Squeezy.
  • Use the license management link in your receipt to deactivate or transfer your license.
  • For refund requests, contact Lemon Squeezy directly or reach out to us at the email below.
