## 5. Legal & Privacy Policy Updates (/zeroed/privacy)

To comply with GDPR and App Store guidelines regarding the collection of email addresses and feedback via the Google Apps Script Webhook.

**Add a new subsection to your Privacy Policy titled: "Beta Feedback Collection"**

* **Data Collected:** "If you participate in our Founder's Beta Exchange, we collect your email address, device OS, and written feedback regarding your experience with the app."
* **Purpose:** "This data is collected strictly for product improvement, bug fixing, and granting your Lifetime License."
* **Data Processing (Webhook):** "To maintain our serverless architecture, this feedback form is processed using a secure Google Apps Script webhook and stored in a private Google Workspace environment (Data Processor). This data is strictly segregated from your financial data."
* **Financial Data Isolation:** "Reiterating our core privacy promise: Your email address and beta feedback are **never** linked to your financial data, accounts, or budgets. Your financial data remains encrypted locally and on your personal Google Drive."
* **Right to Erasure:** "You may request the deletion of your beta feedback and email address at any time by contacting us or using the Account Deletion form."
